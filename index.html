Welcome

When God first gathered his people during the Exodus they went to the Sinai peninsula which was a desolate place, they were there for 40 years.

When they arrived there they spoke only the Egyptian language, then God taught them the language of Heaven, which is only spoken in Heaven, that language is Biblical Hebrew.

Then after 40 years only those who spoke only Biblical Hebrew entered the Kingdom of Israel.

We begin gathering the 144000 elect.The elect will be taught Biblical Hebrew (which is not the modern Hebrew). 

But who is an Elect?, The Elect are chosen by God,and sealed by an Angel in a dream.

In the dream you will see a meteor crossing the sky and hitting the top of a mountain.

Who We Are

The requirements for the Elect:

Sealed – Are sealed by an angel of God in the same way in a dream (the dream must happen after the publication of the First seal).

Follow – Stand on Mount Zion with "a Lamb".

New Song – Sing a new song no one else can sing (requires Biblical Hebrew).

Redeemed – Are redeemed from the earth.

Firstfruits – Are considered the firstfruits of God's harvest.

Work – Have a lot of work to do during the coming tribulation.

If you consider that you are one of the elect then send us the detailed description of your dream and a (1) page resume.

After the 144,000 have been chosen and their names published here, we will meet for the first time, then tasks will be assigned for every Country. 

You must document yourself about the 144,000, first watch these videos taken from the Internet to know more.
