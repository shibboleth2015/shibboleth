Welcome

When God first gathered his people during the Exodus they went to the Sinai peninsula which was a desolate place, they were there for 40 years.

When they arrived there they spoke only the Egyptian language, then God taught them the language of Heaven, which is only spoken in heaven, that language is Biblical Hebrew.

Then after 40 years only those who spoke only Biblical Hebrew entered the Kingdom of Israel.

We now begin gathering the 144,000 spoken of in Revelation 7, if you think you are one of them you first need to learn Biblical Hebrew which is not the modern language spoken in Israel and can not be learned using any available Bible software.

Download the books, begin studying and then if you are one of the Elect you will be sealed by an angel of God, this will happen while you are in your bed.

After you have been sealed you will send us your name and how you were sealed, every one of the elect will be sealed in the same way, then we will publish your name here until the 144,000 are gathered.
